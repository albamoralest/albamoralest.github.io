Alba Morales T.

Computer Science, MSc Infomation Security and Audit.

Alba is a third-year PhD student in the Knowledge Media Institute at the Open University. She is also part of the ISDS (Intelligent Systems and Data Science) research group in KMi focused on the use of semantic technologies to manage large amounts of data.

My research

My research interest is focused on exchanging personal data in the Smart City environment and the effective use of health records by emergency services. My main areas of interest are data privacy and data protection of healthcare data in Smart Cities.

I am currently working on developing an Intelligent System able to act as a mediator between Smart City systems and emergency services, able to identify useful data from citizens' health records and deliver it protecting people privacy.

Background

Alba is a Computer Science graduate and has a master degree in Information Security and Audit from the University of Greenwich.

Before starting her PhD, she worked as Information Security Specialist for several years, specialised in auditing Industrial Control Systems. She has experience working in public and private sector since 2005. As a professional, she has experience as a software developer and manager of implementations of open-source systems for telecommunications and internet services.
